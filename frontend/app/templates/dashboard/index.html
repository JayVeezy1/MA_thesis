ASDF-Dashboard

This dashboard helps you to analyze the fairness of your classifiers on datasets. The fairness is assessed using common fairness criteria (e.g., statistical parity) and the results are visualized in different interactive charts allowing for exploration and understanding of your classifier's behavior.

Upload your dataset

Upload a new dataset (in csv-format) with class labels predicted by your classifier.
Note: The uploaded dataset has to contain one column with the ground-truth class labels as well as one column for the predicted class labels (classification)

Subgroup fairness evaluation

For your uploaded datasets and classification results you can perform a subgroup fairness assessment using an automated process.
The automated process computes a configurable clustering on the uploaded data and derives subgroups from the clusters in two ways:
  • The computed clusters are directly used as subgroups to assess the classification model's fairness on them.
  • From the clusters, patterns are extracted that describe the makeup of the cluster for all the dominant features in the corresponding cluster.
